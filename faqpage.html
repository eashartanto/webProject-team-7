Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

How safe is Toronto?
Toronto is very safe. It is safe to walk the streets at almost any hour of the day or night, with the only caveat being to avoid traditionallly run-down areas after dark.Heed this and little will go wrong.
What is there to do in Toronto?
As we have shown in this blog, there are several activities you can do in Toronto Toronto is a world-class city with a large and varied population. If the world has got it Toronto probably has it too. There are several cultural activities and artistic endeavours are celebrated and there are a wide variety of performances and festivals. If not enough, Toronto boasts an excellent arrays of museums and art galleries. There are several restaurants of different cultures, boating, swimming and fishing on the nearby lakes.For nightlife, there is a great deal from which to choose, with clubs, bars and restaurants open into the early hours of the morning
Do you need a car in Toronto?
In Toronto, there are several place where public transportation like bus , subway and street car are able to reach so, having a car is not a neccesity.Also, the city experiences one of the heaviest traffic, so driving can be more of an inconvenience than anything else.
How are outdoor activities in Toronto?
People who love being outdoor will love the city life in Toronto when the weather isn't too extreme. There are many parks dotted around the city to enjoy a time outside.Toronto Islands are a great place for picnic and a family day during summer.Those who enjoy running or mountain biking can head to Durham Forest, Woodbine lake for watersports like kayaking; and in the winter,Toronto becomes a ice skaters paradise with several ice-skating rinks all over the city

What is the eating out experience in Toronto?
Thanks to the multicultuarl make up of the city,Toronto has thousands of eateries offering a multitude of different cuisines. College Street offers some of the city's best italian restaurant; and Greek food is best enjoyed in the Danforth Area. Authentic Chinese restaurants are located in ChinaTown around Dundas Street and Spadina Avenue.Gerard Street is a good option if looking for Indian cuisines.
How is the Arts and Culture scenary in Toronto?

The scene is incredibly diverse in Toronto.While you can marvel at masterpieces like the Art Gallery of Ontario, it is also possible to appreciate the city's artistic community while out and about thanks to the streetart that is displayed al over Toronto. Historic music halls like Massey Hall and the Phoenix Concert Theatre host regular music events. The city's theatre district is centered on King Street West.
