OUR TEAM

We are a team of four international Humber College students who started Toronto Today as a simple web project. Since we are newcomers to Toronto, we know how hard it may be to adapt to new environments.Seeing no alternatives to an easy-to-use news and entertainment website exclusively covering what's happening in the city that never sleeps, we decided to create our own.Now we strive to keep you up to date with all the biggest news that are happening in the city.
